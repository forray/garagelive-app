PROMOTION

OUR PROMOTION VISION

At the beginning of the path of becoming a Star, everything is up to You. You need to build your fan crowd, You need to communicate with them on social pages like Facebook or Twitter, encourage them to listen from your tunes. There isn’t any record company who gives You a contract with full of money if You don’t have fans. Fans will buy your tracks, they will listen your video clipp on YouTube, and they will share your songs with their friends, so actually they will increase the number of fans!

So for start, we give You some advice how to be a Star!

PROMOTION PYRAMID

Our promotion pyramid is based on your sellings, and You can sell more if You start to communicate with your Fans! If You sell more than 5000 track, we will offer You a special VIP contract with lot’s of good things like CD/Vinly productions, Interviews, Concerts etc. This offer is belong to all online store where your song released, and the together 5000, not per each. Check out our Promotion Pyramid and set your goal!



5 STEP TO SUCCESS

If You follow these steps maybe oneday You will be a good musician, but it’s not that easy for everyone, keep on eye on your goals, and find your happiness on music and with music. We will give you advices what should You change or do, we will manage your portfolio and help fo find your way and be more & more successful.



MONTHLY COMPETITIONS

Every month we check all new song at GarageLive, and check which has the highest like number, and this song owner will get $200 advert coupon, which is for promoting this song on different online stores. All the songs will participate in the competition, so you don’t have to do anything to be in this competitions, just create an awesome song. Competitions will start at the end of this summer.

MUSICIAN CONTESTS

These contest are constantly present on the site, in different styles, with different musicians/bands. Everyone can apply for these contests, just need to sign up, and upload track and that’s it.

TALENT SCOUT PROGRAM

Here you can know more about GarageLive Managers. These guys are coming from different music tribes, from electric to metal music, they know everything about how to help you or your band to be more & more successful. They have contacts to a lot of music magazines, websites and other music companys to give you a possibility to show these guys how good you are.

GOLD MEMBERSHIP

If you’re a GOLD member at GarageLive, you already has a Personal Garagelive Manager! If you’re FREE or PRO member, then you still have a chance that some of our managers will find you, because they are checking all project on GarageLive all the time.
